<p><p>The Outpatient Infusion Therapy Service market is categorized into two main types: long-term therapy and short-term therapy. Long-term therapy involves ongoing treatments for chronic conditions, requiring repeated infusions over extended periods, often focusing on diseases like rheumatoid arthritis or multiple sclerosis. In contrast, short-term therapy is typically aimed at acute conditions that necessitate infusions for a limited duration, such as antibiotic treatment for infections or chemotherapy sessions. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing patient care outside traditional hospital settings.</p></p>
