<p><ul><li>PTC Heater</li><li>Heat Pump Heater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Heating System market predominantly features two types: PTC heaters and heat pump heaters. PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) heaters utilize ceramic elements that warm up quickly, providing consistent heat. They are effective for immediate cabin warmth but consume more energy. In contrast, heat pump heaters transfer heat from the outside air to heat the vehicle, functioning efficiently in various temperatures and using less energy. Both systems cater to enhancing passenger comfort while optimizing energy usage in electric vehicles.</p></p>
