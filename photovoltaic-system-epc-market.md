<p><p>The Photovoltaic System EPC market is divided into two main types: Centralized and Distributed Photovoltaic EPC. Centralized Photovoltaic EPC involves large-scale solar power plants, typically located in remote areas, where energy is generated and transmitted to the grid. This model benefits from economies of scale. In contrast, Distributed Photovoltaic EPC focuses on smaller installations, such as rooftop solar systems, closer to the point of consumption, which enhances energy efficiency and reduces transmission losses. Both models cater to diverse energy needs.</p></p>
