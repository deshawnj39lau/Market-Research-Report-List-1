<p><ul><li>Electronic Toll Collection</li><li>All Electronic Toll Collection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Pay As You Go (PAYG) road charging market encompasses various systems for charging users based on road usage. Electronic Toll Collection (ETC) systems use transponders to automatically charge vehicles at toll booths, allowing for smoother traffic flow. In contrast, All Electronic Toll Collection (AETC) operates without physical toll booths, relying on cameras and sensors to capture vehicle data for charging. Both systems aim to enhance efficiency, reduce congestion, and ensure fair payment based on actual road usage.</p></p>
