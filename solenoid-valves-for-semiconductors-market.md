<p><p>Solenoid valves for semiconductors play a crucial role in controlling fluid and gas flow in various applications. They are categorized primarily by configuration: 2-way valves allow for simple on/off control, while 3-way valves enable more complex routing of fluids. By product type, solenoid valves can be classified as normally closed or normally open; normally closed valves remain shut until electrically activated, while normally open valves are open and close upon activation. These classifications are essential for optimizing semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
