<p><p>The enteric capsules market comprises three main types: Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC), Gelatin, and Vegetable-based capsules. HPMC capsules are popular due to their plant-derived nature and suitability for vegetarians, offering controlled release of contents in the intestine. Gelatin capsules, commonly derived from animal sources, are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and quick dissolving properties. Vegetable capsules, made from plant polysaccharides, cater to a growing demand for vegan and allergen-free options, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
