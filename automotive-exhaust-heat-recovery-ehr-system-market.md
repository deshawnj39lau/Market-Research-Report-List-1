<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery (EHR) System market encompasses several types of technologies. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) systems capture waste heat from exhaust to improve efficiency. Rankine cycle systems convert heat into mechanical energy, enhancing overall power output. Thermoelectric generators utilize temperature differences to generate electricity directly from waste heat. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) incorporates electric generators with turbochargers, using exhaust energy to drive additional turbines, thus improving vehicle performance and efficiency. Each type aims to maximize energy utilization and reduce emissions.</p></p>
