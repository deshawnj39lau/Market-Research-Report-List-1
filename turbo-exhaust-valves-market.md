<p><p>The turbo exhaust valves market is divided into two primary types: flange and welded. Flange turbo exhaust valves are designed with a flat rim that allows for easy attachment to other engine components, facilitating straightforward installation and maintenance. In contrast, welded turbo exhaust valves are permanently affixed to exhaust systems, ensuring a more secure and durable connection. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance requirements, installation preferences, and system compatibility, influencing their adoption in various automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
