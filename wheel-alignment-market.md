<p><p>The wheel alignment market encompasses three primary types: Thrust Alignment, Two-Wheel Alignment, and Four-Wheel Alignment. Thrust Alignment focuses on aligning the rear wheels to the front wheels, ensuring that the vehicle tracks straight. Two-Wheel Alignment involves adjusting the front wheels only, suitable for vehicles with a solid rear axle. Four-Wheel Alignment, the most comprehensive option, adjusts all four wheels, ensuring optimal handling and tire wear. These alignment types cater to varying vehicle needs and alignment requirements.</p></p>
