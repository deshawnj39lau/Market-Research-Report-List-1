<p><p>The rearview mirror replacement market comprises primarily two types: CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras offer high image quality and exceptional low-light performance, making them ideal for capturing clear footage in dim conditions. Conversely, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras are more energy-efficient and cost-effective, providing decent image quality and faster processing speeds. Both technologies are increasingly integrated into modern vehicles, enhancing safety and visibility by offering advanced rearview functionalities and driver assistance features.</p></p>
